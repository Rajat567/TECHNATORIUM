"Work on Ideology

Our team Technatorium basically works on four I's ,
Ideas Improvisation Innovation and Implentation
just like aur slogan says , we work on our ideas and implement them in our technical work . Work on Ideology ..Implement in Technology.
Since the team has started , we had worked on several projects . Like , our first project was an IoT Based Air Quality monitoring module using LoRa Technology , With the help of this networking technology, we can place sensors in locations without the need of an internet/wifi connectivity. We can receive the data via theLoRa gateway and then it can be send to internet via MQTT. By this way we can monitor the real time sensor data from anywhere.We presented this project in MAKATHON 2021 , and were the
Champion of Champions.
